About Ooty

Ooty is one of the most beautiful hill stations in Tamil Nadu. It is famous for its cool weather, lush tea plantations, beautiful gardens, and breathtaking mountain views. It is the perfect destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

🏞️ Top Places to Visit

  1. Ooty Lake
  2. Nilgiri Hills
  3. Government Botanical Garden
  4. Doddabetta Peak
  5. Rose Garden
  6. Tea Plantations
